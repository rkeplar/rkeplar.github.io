Data Analytics Portfolio

Rachel Keplar

For my first Tableau project I created a World Happiness workbook. I began with visualizing the happiness scores on a map as colors diverging between red and green and compared them to each country's GDP, represented by the size of the circle.

Next, I compared the same fields on a heat map.

The bigger the square, the bigger the GDP, and the darker the square the higher the happiness.

Finally, I compared the World Happiness and GDP scores with the Healthy Life Expectancy for each country on a scatterplot. The line of best fit shows a positive correlation between happiness and higher GDP. The colors show that the higher the GDP, the better the Life Expectancy as well.
